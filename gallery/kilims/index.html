Kilim Rugs in Portland, Oregon

Kilim rugs are flat-woven, pileless textiles known for vivid geometry, reversible construction, and versatile everyday use. Our Portland showroom offers kilims that add color, movement, and artisan texture to entryways, dining areas, bedrooms, and casual living spaces.
